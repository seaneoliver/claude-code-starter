## Career Development Roadmap

### Current Role Mastery (0-12 months)

**Quarter 1: Foundation & Visibility**
- [ ] Establish credibility with key stakeholders
- [ ] Deliver 2-3 high-impact insights that drive business decisions
- [ ] Build relationships with peer Business Analytics Leads
- [ ] Document and optimize one repeatable process
- [ ] Present insights to leadership (QBR/MBR level)

**Quarter 2-4: Influence & Scale**
- [ ] Lead cross-functional initiative requiring multiple stakeholder alignment
- [ ] Mentor junior team members or peers
- [ ] Identify and implement process improvement with measurable impact
- [ ] Present to executive leadership (Director+ level)
- [ ] Develop thought leadership (internal blog, presentation, training)

### Advanced Impact (12-24 months)

**Strategic Contributions**
- [ ] Own end-to-end strategy for a business function's analytics needs
- [ ] Drive organizational change through data-driven insights
- [ ] Build or significantly enhance a capability (tool, process, methodology)
- [ ] Establish yourself as go-to expert in a domain area
- [ ] Influence roadmap and investment decisions

**Leadership Expansion**
- [ ] Lead team of Business Analytics professionals (formal or informal)
- [ ] Partner with engineering on product/platform enhancements
- [ ] Represent analytics in strategic planning processes
- [ ] Build external visibility (conference speaking, community leadership)

### Promotion Readiness (18-30 months)

**Senior/Principal Criteria**
- [ ] Consistent track record of business impact across multiple areas
- [ ] Recognized thought leader in analytics domain
- [ ] Demonstrated ability to operate at executive level
- [ ] Built scalable solutions used beyond immediate team
- [ ] Mentored others to success
- [ ] Shaped organizational direction through insights and influence

## Success Metrics & Tracking

### Impact Indicators

**Business Impact**
- Number of strategic decisions influenced by your insights
- Time/cost saved through process improvements
- Revenue or efficiency gains attributable to your work
- Scope of stakeholders regularly consuming your deliverables

**Technical Impact**
- Datasets/dashboards created and actively used
- Processes automated or optimized
- Quality metrics (accuracy, timeliness, adoption)
- Technical debt reduced or prevented

**Leadership Impact**
- Stakeholder satisfaction and trust level
- Number of people mentored or influenced
- Cross-team initiatives led or contributed to
- Recognition from leadership (awards, visibility, opportunities)

### Quarterly Review Questions

**Business Outcomes**
1. What business problems did I solve this quarter?
2. How did my work influence key decisions or strategy?
3. What friction did I eliminate for stakeholders?
4. Where did I create lasting value beyond individual deliverables?

**Capability Growth**
1. What new technical skills did I develop?
2. How did I expand my business acumen?
3. Where did I stretch beyond my comfort zone?
4. What mistakes did I learn from?

**Relationships & Influence**
1. Which new relationships did I build?
2. How did I strengthen existing partnerships?
3. Where did I successfully influence without authority?
4. What feedback did I receive and act on?

**Strategic Positioning**
1. How am I known across the organization?
2. What unique value do I bring?
3. Where am I building thought leadership?
4. How am I preparing for the next level?

## Growth Mindset Practices

### Weekly Reflection (15 minutes)

**What went well?**
- Capture wins, learnings, and moments of impact
- Note positive feedback or recognition
- Identify what to repeat or amplify

**What could improve?**
- Analyze setbacks or frustrations
- Consider alternative approaches
- Identify skill gaps exposed

**What's my learning edge?**
- Note areas where I felt stretched
- Capture new perspectives gained
- Identify emerging interests or opportunities

### Monthly Deep Dive (60 minutes)

**Impact Review**
- Review deliverables and outcomes
- Assess stakeholder feedback
- Measure against quarterly goals
- Document key decisions influenced

**Skill Development**
- Evaluate progress on development goals
- Identify new capabilities to build
- Seek feedback from trusted colleagues
- Plan next month's learning focus

**Network & Influence**
- Map stakeholder relationships
- Assess influence and trust levels
- Identify gaps in network
- Plan relationship-building activities

**Strategic Positioning**
- Review career roadmap progress
- Assess visibility and reputation
- Identify opportunities to increase impact
- Update career development plan

## Core Principles

**Career development is not separate from daily work.**
Every project is an opportunity to build skills, relationships, and reputation. Every challenge is a learning opportunity. Every stakeholder interaction shapes how you're known.

**Focus on impact, not activity.**
You'll advance faster by solving important problems exceptionally well than by being busy with low-impact work.

**Build your brand intentionally.**
How people describe you when you're not in the room determines your opportunities. Shape that narrative through consistent, visible impact.

**Invest in relationships.**
Your network determines your access to opportunities, information, and influence. Build genuine partnerships.
