## Core Competencies Self-Assessment

Four domains required for role mastery. Rate each at one of four levels: Foundational, Proficient, Expert, Thought Leader.

### 1. Technical Excellence
**Skills:** Power BI development, data modeling and architecture (OCDM, Azure Data Lake, SQL, Cosmos DB, SharePoint), end-to-end insights lifecycle ownership, quality assurance, scalable process design, emerging technologies evaluation.

- [ ] Foundational: Can execute with guidance
- [ ] Proficient: Can execute independently
- [ ] Expert: Can mentor others and set standards
- [ ] Thought Leader: Driving innovation and best practices

---

### 2. Business Acumen
**Skills:** Connecting dots across value cycles, hypothesis-driven problem solving, business problem definition and scoping, stakeholder prioritization and alignment, ROB process understanding, cross-functional impact assessment.

- [ ] Foundational: Understanding business context
- [ ] Proficient: Contributing to strategy discussions
- [ ] Expert: Shaping business direction through insights
- [ ] Thought Leader: Influencing organizational strategy

---

### 3. Leadership & Influence
**Skills:** Executive communication (technical and non-technical audiences), cross-team collaboration and boundary spanning, consultative orientation and trust building, team leadership and partner coordination, conflict resolution and ambiguity navigation.

- [ ] Foundational: Building relationships
- [ ] Proficient: Influencing peers and direct stakeholders
- [ ] Expert: Influencing across org boundaries
- [ ] Thought Leader: Shaping culture and operating models

---

### 4. Operational Excellence
**Skills:** High-quality, repeatable deliverables, standards and best practices enforcement, issue investigation and resolution partnership, continuous learning and capability enhancement, bias for action and results orientation.

- [ ] Foundational: Meeting basic delivery expectations
- [ ] Proficient: Consistently delivering high-quality work
- [ ] Expert: Setting standards and improving processes
- [ ] Thought Leader: Transforming operational models
