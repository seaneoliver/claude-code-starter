# Career Conversation Templates

Templates for 1:1s, skip-levels, peer relationships, and first coaching session activation.

---

## 1:1 with Manager — Development Focus

**Opening:**
"I want to ensure I'm developing the right capabilities to maximize my impact in this role and position myself for future growth. Can we discuss my development priorities?"

**Discussion Points:**
- Share self-assessment of competency levels
- Review impact metrics from recent work
- Discuss skill gaps and development opportunities
- Align on priority growth areas
- Identify stretch assignments or projects
- Request specific feedback on leadership presence

**Closing:**
"What's the one area where increased capability would most accelerate my impact right now?"

---

## Skip-Level — Strategic Alignment

**Opening:**
"I'd value your perspective on how analytics can drive the most impact for our organization's strategic priorities."

**Discussion Points:**
- Share recent high-impact work and outcomes
- Ask about strategic priorities and pain points
- Explore where analytics could unlock value
- Discuss organizational challenges you could help address
- Seek advice on building influence and visibility

**Closing:**
"What would success look like if analytics were operating at its full potential in our organization?"

---

## Peer Relationships — Collaboration

**Opening:**
"I'm looking to understand how I can better support [their function/team] through analytics and insights."

**Discussion Points:**
- Understand their key challenges and priorities
- Share relevant capabilities and past wins
- Explore collaboration opportunities
- Offer to tackle a pain point together
- Build mutual understanding of constraints and needs

**Closing:**
"What's one analytics challenge that, if solved, would make your work significantly easier?"

## First Coaching Session — Self-Activation

Complete this to establish your career development baseline.

### Self-Assessment (30 minutes)

**1. Current State**
- Rate yourself on the four core competencies (1-10)
- List your top 3 strengths in this role
- Identify your top 3 development areas
- Note recent wins and proud moments

**2. Future Vision**
- Where do you want to be in 2 years?
- What role or responsibilities excite you?
- What impact do you want to have?
- What reputation do you want to build?

**3. Gap Analysis**
- What skills separate current from future state?
- What experiences do you need?
- What relationships do you need to build?
- What mindsets need to shift?

**4. 90-Day Priorities**
- Choose 1-2 development focus areas
- Identify 2-3 high-impact projects or deliverables
- List key relationships to build or strengthen
- Set learning goals (courses, certifications, reading)

### Next Steps
1. Schedule monthly career development check-ins with yourself
2. Share development priorities with your manager
3. Identify one stretch assignment or opportunity
4. Find one mentor or peer coach
5. Begin tracking your impact metrics
