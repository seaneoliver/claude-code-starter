# {{YOUR_LEVEL}}/{{YOUR_NEXT_LEVEL}} Framework

## {{YOUR_LEVEL}} Level Expectations

At {{YOUR_LEVEL}}, you're expected to operate with **significant autonomy** and **cross-team influence**. The role requires moving beyond individual execution to **driving adoption**, **creating efficiency at scale**, and **shaping how analytics work gets done** across multiple areas.

### {{YOUR_LEVEL}} Performance Standards

**Business Understanding (Breadth & Anticipation)**
- Build understanding across multiple business areas, not just your immediate domain
- Link internal business topics to relevant external trends
- Anticipate business problems before they're escalated
- Understand how external market/industry trends impact internal business decisions

**Data Strategy & Integration**
- Leverage appropriate types and sources of data/metrics for diverse use cases
- Translate business needs into data requirements independently
- Evaluate sufficiency of data sources and identify integration opportunities
- Anticipate and address data quality gaps proactively, partnering with data providers and other teams

**Communication & Storytelling**
- Develop presentations that tell stories, not just show data
- Create diverse formats: dashboards, reports, visualizations, self-service platforms, slides, talking points
- Highlight relevant insights that drive understanding and action
- Tailor communication to audience (technical vs. non-technical, executive vs. operational)

**Impact Assessment & Decision Support**
- Determine evaluation frameworks for assessing impact of business, product, and operational decisions
- Identify relevant factors and data types to include in impact assessments
- Provide assessments that inform strategic decisions, not just report metrics

**Relationship & Influence (Cross-Team)**
- Leverage working relationships within and across teams to ensure quality
- Drive adoption of tools and processes that facilitate insight generation
- Partner with data providers, engineering, and other analytics teams

**Operational Efficiency & Scale**
- Identify and promote methods that create efficiency in analytics and reporting
- Build solutions that are reusable, discoverable, self-service
- Direct stakeholders toward meaningful interpretation of data
- Focus on outputs that drive business decisions and impact, not just activity
