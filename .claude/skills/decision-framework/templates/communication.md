# Communication Templates

## To Those Affected

```
Subject: Decision: [What was decided]

What: [Decision in 1 sentence]

Why: [Rationale - business value]

When: [Implementation timeline]

Impact on You: [Specific changes for this audience]

What to Do: [Any actions required]

Questions: [How to get more info]
```

---

## To Leadership

```
Subject: Decision: [What was decided] - [Status]

Decision: [Clear statement]

Rationale: [Brief - 2-3 sentences]

Stakeholder Alignment: [Who's on board]

Implementation: [Timeline and plan]

Risks: [Key risks and mitigation]

FYI or need approval: [Which one]
```
